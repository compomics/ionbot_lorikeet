ionbotTM AI models predict peptide behaviour to match MS2 spectra with peptides

Both MS2 spectrum predictions from MS2PIP and LC retention time predictions from DeepLC are exploited by ionbotTM during MS2 spectrum matching.

We have shown that the information computed from the AI models substantially increases the confidence in the peptide identifications compared to other OMS engines.

Our data-driven AI approach also allows for optimizing your search for specific experimental conditions. For instance, ionbotTM uses AI models trained on TMT labeled data to more accurately analyse your TMT labeled data.
